Between September 18, 2019, and May 18, 2023, Samir Libari (Chico Loco 40) underwent a profound personal and psychological crucible that ultimately laid the foundation for the CL40 World digital ecosystem

The Legal Narrative and The Royal Pardon

Originally facing a five-year sentence, Libari’s legal counsel successfully secured a reduction to one year on appeal. Throughout this high-pressure incarceration period, Libari demonstrated absolute resilience, maintaining an impeccable behavioral record despite severe institutional hardships. The definitive turning point of his confinement occurred on May 18, 2023, just four months before completing his full sentence. Recognizing his exemplary conduct, His Majesty King Mohammed VI graciously granted Libari a Royal Pardon, commuting the final three months of his sentence. This sovereign act of clemency marked the official conclusion of this chapter and initiated his immediate professional rebirth
